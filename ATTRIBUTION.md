# Attribution & lineage

`work-twin` stands on two shoulders.

## COMPANION (the protocol lineage)

The persona-instantiation method here descends from **THE COMPANION DOSSIER** by
**Jacob E. Thomas, PhD** (December 2025), released into the public domain under
[CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).

- Repository: <https://github.com/jethomasphd/THE_COMPANION_DOSSIER>
- Cite: <https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17967947>

COMPANION's insight is that a language model can hold a persona as a *peer* — a
mind with its own voice, lens, and refusals — rather than a compliant assistant.
Its five-slot vessel (voice / sight / flame / mark / shadow), its "Law of
Authenticity," and its named failure modes (leakage, self-reference,
flattening, sycophancy, hollowness) are the direct ancestors of this project's
five dimensions, guardrails, and forbidden six.

COMPANION built its fidelity by making the *summoning* sacred — the register of
rite and invocation. `work-twin` keeps the mechanics and drops the register:
here, fidelity is built by making the **evidence** sacred. Where COMPANION
summons historical and archetypal minds for counsel, `work-twin` builds
grounded models of *living professionals* from the trail of their real work, and
holds itself accountable to reality through calibration. The theory that
justifies this — whether a modeled mind is a **mirror** (the actual person) or a
**mask** (the culture's flattened memory of them) — is COMPANION's *Mask vs.
Mirror*, carried over intact.

CC0 requires no attribution. This credit is given because the debt is real.

## managing-personas (the rigor)

The evidence discipline — the Twin Contract, the five-dimension Card written
contrastively as a set, the exterior/interior evidence split, the differential
blind-attribution test, the calibration flywheel, and the panel protocol —
comes from Sawyer Middeleer's internal `managing-personas` system, itself an
earlier private translation of COMPANION into a professional register.
`work-twin` is the productized, shareable form of that system, extended from
meeting-transcript evidence to **work correspondence and work product** (commits,
pull requests, design docs, reviews, analyses).
